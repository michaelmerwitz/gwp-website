About

Who we are

Greenwave builds large solar farms that provide clean electricity to the grid. Our solar farms bring green, renewable energy at a scale that displaces significant dirty power to make a measurable impact in the fight against climate change.

Our mission is to accelerate the deployment of renewable energy by allowing communities to prosper from our solar farms. We work with landowners and communities to locate, permit, construct, and operate our solar farms, creating tangible economic benefits to stakeholders wherever we go. We become a part of the communities that host our solar farms. Our projects provide long-term revenues to landowners and local governments. Our solar farms are built with land preservation in mind by using construction practices that result in minimal visual or environmental impact. We have a track record of working with communities and decision makers to achieve favorable outcomes.

Why We Are

Our vision is to lead an immediate transition to a greener future and improve communities. The need to address climate change is accelerating with each passing moment, and Greenwave incentivizes communities to join others in the fight to prevent the catastrophic damage and extraordinary costs of climate change that our future generations will face by doing nothing. Our team is committed to combating the impacts of climate change through the deployment of renewable energy.
